How It Works

🔍 Smart Aggregation

Fetches from 21 sources including HackerNews, Reddit, ArXiv, and top AI newsletters. Retries with exponential backoff on failures.

📊 Interest Scoring

Three-tier keyword scoring prioritizes enterprise AI, coding tools, agents, and thought leadership. Penalizes benchmarks and academic plumbing.

🕐 Freshness First

Articles sorted newest-first after quality filtering. 48-hour freshness window ensures you only see current news.

🎙️ Podcast Support

Download, transcribe, and summarize podcast episodes locally using yt-dlp and faster-whisper. No cloud APIs required.
